What's the game about?

Redo is a platformer game about collecting abilities.
Each time you beat a level, you gain a new ability. The end of a level is a new beginning and with each ability a new path opens up. The goal is to unlock every ability and use them to beat the final level. Can you beat Redo?

Why did I make this project?

During the Brackeys game jam 2023.1 the theme was "An end is a new beginning". I had joined the game jam at that time and made a game idea for it. I never finished the game and only spent a couple days on it.

During my internship I started a new project, with the same game idea. This project is called Redo and I made a game design document for it which contains everything.

What did I learn?

I got better at making game design documents and working with the agile workway. Because I worked in sprints, I managed to complete a level every month.
This includes playtesting and planning ahead.

During the development of Redo, I got better at game design. When I made the fake floor puzzle in the first level, I didn't like how it turned out to be. I completely remade the puzzle and even added a "tutorial" (if you fall, a text explanation appears). If you are struggling with this puzzle, the game helps you too. After failing the puzzle 5 times, the correct tiles will turn green once stepped on.

Other Information

Redo took me 3 months to make in Unity (around 40 hours per week) and you can play it on Windows.
